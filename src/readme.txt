To run the current program, do the following. 

Run the Login class
When prompted, press 2 to create an account
Enter your desired username and password
Once done, press 1 to Login using your desired username or password.

When you are logged in:
Press and enter 1 to begin "mining" a block
Press and enter 2 to transfer currency to another user
Press and enter 3 to print your current amount of currency
Press and enter 4 to logout of your curent account
